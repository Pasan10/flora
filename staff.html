After-Sales Support

  • 1. Follow-up with Customers: Send a thank-you message via email, SMS, or WhatsApp after purchase. Ask for feedback and offer a discount for their next order.
  • 2. Handle Complaints Promptly: Offer a replacement or refund for damaged or wilted flowers. Train staff to handle customer issues politely and efficiently.
  • 3. Care Tips for Flowers: Provide care instructions with every order. Send reminders via email or social media on how to keep flowers fresh longer.
  • 4. Loyalty & Rewards Program: Offer points for every purchase that can be redeemed for discounts. Provide exclusive deals for returning customers.
  • 5. Personalized Recommendations: Use customer purchase history to suggest flowers for upcoming occasions. Offer subscription-based flower delivery services.
  • 6. Social Media & Online Engagement: Encourage customers to share photos of their flowers and tag your shop. Run contests or giveaways to keep them engaged.
  • 7. Seasonal Promotions & Reminders: Send reminders for anniversaries, birthdays, and special occasions. Offer early bird discounts for events like Valentine’s Day and Mother’s Day.
